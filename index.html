CN8 is a Foundational Nutrition supplement made powerfully simple and convenient. While you can take CN8 at any time, we recommend taking CN8 first thing in the morning on an empty stomach for optimal absorption. To use, add 8 to 10 ounces of cold water to a glass or your CN8 shaker bottle. You can adjust the amount of water-based on your taste preference. Then, add 1 scoop of CN8 powder. Shake or stir until fully blended. Drink and enjoy! Don’t forget – your CN8 pouch must be stored in a refrigerator after opening.
